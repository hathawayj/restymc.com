About the studio

Yoga is for every body!

Whether small, large, male, female, flexible or inflexible, yoga is for you. During each practice you will learn to listen to the needs of your body as you move through the sequences.

With many props and modifications the asanas (poses) are accessible to everyone, making this a safe place for your body, mind, and spirit.
